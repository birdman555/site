Restaurante Villa Magna

On the corner of Paseo de la Castellana and Calle Ortega y Gasset the Restaurante Villa Magna continues to innovate.

With Amalio Prados as Executive Chef, the Restaurante Villa Magna strives to create the finest gastronomic experiences. With a philosophy focused on absolute respect for local product, quality is the foundation stone: always using the finest available product to compliment the changing seasons.

The best way to round off the culinary delights on offer at the Restaurante Villa Magna is by sampling its extensive dessert menu. The patisserie at the Villa Magna comes up with marvellous creations, made possible by the ability to combine new textures and flavours with classic recipes. All the sweets are created by very talented professionals.

The wine list is extensive and features a large selection of wine by the glass as well as by the bottle.

The elegant yet casual ambience of the restaurant and the terraza over the summer months are ideally suited for casual and business dinners.

Restaurante Villa Magna Cancellation Policy

Reservations for a party of 8 persons or more will be requested to guarantee the reservation at the time of booking with a credit card. Confirmation of the reservation will be sent to the guests email address. If you wish to cancel your reservation, please call the Restaurant +34 91 587 1234.
